Shadowland: Beginnings: Week 18

Genesis 13

The Testing of Lot

    13:2 The riches acquired in Egypt will "pierce through with many sorrows" - 1 Timothy 6:10 (as will the handmaiden, Hagar).
  • 13:3 "went on his journeys" - retraced his steps, back to where he started, "where his tent had been at the beginning."
  • 13:4 "the altar which he had made there at first" - emphasizing that the sojourn in Egypt was a total loss.
  • 13:7 Unbelievers observed the strife between believers.
  • 13:10 "lifted up his eyes" - as in Genesis 3, the eyes are a key gate of temptation. 3:6, Joshua 7:21, 1 John 2:16 "like the land of Egypt" - Lot is still longing for Egypt.
  • 13:13 "sinners before the LORD" - in God's face, as with Nimrod.
  • 13:14 After Abram takes another step to fully obey God's instruction, God again gives him more light.
  • 13:17 God gives Abram a vision
  • 13:18 "Mamre" - vision or fatness. Hebron - fellowship. Fellowship is first mentioned when Abram fully obeys.

The Descent of Lot

  • Longing for the riches of Egypt 13:10
  • Lifted up his eyes toward a land like Egypt
  • Dwelt in the cities of the plain. 13:12
  • Pitched his tent toward Sodom.
  • Dwelt in Sodom 14:2
  • Delivered from Sodom in Genesis 14, but returned to Sodom Amos 4:11
  • Elder of Sodom 19:1
  • Daughters married men of Sodom.
  • Lack of fruit 18:32
  • Vexed in soul 2 Peter 2:7-8
  • Powerless testimony 19:14
  • Still attached to Sodom 19:16
  • Snatched as a branch from the fire. Zechariah 3, 1 Corinthians 3:15
