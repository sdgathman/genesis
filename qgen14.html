Week 14 Quiz

The New World

  1. What are some ways in which Noah in the new world mirrors Adam in his new world?
  2. What are some ways in which the emergence of the new world mirrors the emergence of the previous world?
  3. As far as the text of Genesis is concerned, could Noah's flood have been a local event?
  4. Was the curse on Caanan a justification for slavery in America?
  5. Who did God make a covenant with after the flood?
  6. What important human institution did God establish after the flood? Why do you think it was needed?
  7. How did the human diet change after the flood?
  8. Which son of Noah occupies most of the earth? What does his name mean?
