Jacob's Ladder

  1. What was the name of the city near where Jacob lay down to sleep?
  2. What was the ladder bridging heaven and earth that Jacob saw in his dream?
  3. What did Jacob change the name of the city to after his dream?
  4. What was the significance of the name change?
  5. What is the significance of Jacob's descendants becoming as the "dust of the earth", instead of the "stars of heaven" as with Abraham?
  6. Did God have any conditions for his promises to Jacob?
  7. What requirement did Jacob add to God's promise, and why?
